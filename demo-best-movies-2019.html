Best Films
of 2019

According to Mike Mai

2019 brought an epic end to an decade

Marvel created the biggest movie-going event in history; Sandler, Murphy, and J-Lo became hot topics for multiple film awards; the breathtaking Keanu took over everything; Marty made a 160 million Netflix film; Tarantino delivered a love letter to the old Hollywood; & Shia proved to be a serious filmmaker.

Creating a top-ten or even top-five list was impossible, however, the top three spots are uncompromising for me. The following films are pure cinema magic.

№ 3

Parasite

Bong Joon-ho

The Global Crowd-pleaser

The South Korean director Bong Joon-ho’s dark comedy thriller made a huge splash in his hometown’s box office and that hype had spread all over the world. Not only was it a financial success, it was also praised critically as it snatched a win at The Golden Globe and multiple nominations for The Oscars.
